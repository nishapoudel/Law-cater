Meet Our Experienced Team

Life is synonymous to stories. And stories belong to the ordinary people just as much as they are highlighted to be about the ones in the limelight. Our objective as 'Lawcaters' is to bring out the unheard and underrepresented stories of newness and timeless truths, of ecstasies and pains, of pioneering and stereotypes, to a platform visible to more common sharers of such experiences. Along with serving stories, we intend to infuse law wherever it's able to address issues circumventing the stories.In today's stage of civilization, as much synonymous life is to stories, it is to law too. So you can expect doses of laymen stories from us and to be weaved along as we try to opine socio-legal solutions to very many issues we as a society do not address in general.
